# DrinkX S400 — Product Description

> **Product type:** Complete automated self-service beverage complex
> **Target segment:** High-traffic retail locations (supermarket chains, fuel stations, large-format retail)

---

## Marketing Description

> Deliver a complete beverage menu at retail scale — without a barista, without compromise.

DrinkX S400 is a fully automated self-service beverage complex engineered for high-traffic retail locations. It produces up to 110 drinks per hour from a menu spanning coffee-based drinks, tea and functional beverages — with up to 4 milk types and 10 syrup variants available simultaneously through a single touch-screen ordering interface, with no staff required at the point of service.

**USP:**
> Up to 110 drinks/hour, 4 milk types, 10 syrups — one autonomous self-service station managed entirely through DrinkX Cloud, with zero barista required.

---

## Menu Configuration

| Category | Capacity |
|---|---|
| Milk types | Up to 4 |
| Milk volume | Up to 19 L |
| Coffee concentrate | Up to 12 L (up to 4 x 3L Bag-in-Box) |
| Syrups | Up to 10 x 1.5L bottles |

## Technical Specifications

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| Productivity | Up to 110 drinks/hour |
| Water | Up to 19 L tank OR mains connection |
| Drain | Up to 10 L tank OR sewage connection |
| Autonomy | Up to 110 drinks per full load |
| Dimensions | 1200 x 800 x 1090 mm |
| Power supply | 3-phase, 400V, 50Hz |
| Power consumption | 5-7.2 kW |
| Weight | Max 250 kg |
| Operating conditions | Indoors, +10C to +40C |

## Placement Requirements

- Indoor installation, protected from moisture and external factors
- Level horizontal surface
- 3-phase 400V 50Hz electrical connection
- Water supply and drainage preferred; autonomous operation available within tank volumes
- Stable internet connection recommended for DrinkX Cloud telemetry
